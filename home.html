💼 About Me

I am a software developer and machine learning enthusiast with a strong foundation in data analysis and statistical modeling. My work centers around transforming raw data into meaningful insights and building practical systems that solve real-world problems.

I have hands-on experience with Python for data analysis, model development, and backend engineering. I regularly work with tools such as Pandas, NumPy, and scikit-learn, and I am comfortable implementing and evaluating machine learning models, including linear regression and related optimization techniques.

Beyond data science, I build backend systems using Django and REST APIs, focusing on clean architecture, scalability, and performance. I also work with SQL, Excel, and Power BI for data querying, visualization, and business intelligence reporting.

I am deeply interested in understanding the mathematical foundations behind models, not just using them as black boxes. Whether analyzing regression assumptions, handling edge cases in data preprocessing, or optimizing model performance, I approach problems with curiosity and precision.

Currently, I am focused on strengthening my expertise in advanced regression techniques, loss functions, and model evaluation, while continuously building projects that bridge theory and practical application.
